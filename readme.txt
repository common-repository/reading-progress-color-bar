The Reading Progress Color Bar adds a stylish top-bar that shows reading progress, enhancing user experience on your pages and posts.

== Description ==

**Reading Progress Color Bar** enhances user engagement by adding a sleek progress bar at the top of your browser window as readers scroll through your content. This visual indicator not only improves user experience by showing how much content remains but also encourages visitors to continue reading. The progress bar can be easily customized to fit your site's design through the plugin settings, allowing you to change its color with just a few clicks.

**Features:**

- Dynamic progress bar that shows the reading progress of posts and pages.
- Simple color customization options to match your website’s theme.
- Lightweight and fast-loading, ensuring it doesn’t slow down your site.
- Compatible with most WordPress themes and devices.

== Installation ==

1. Download and extract the `reading-progress-color-bar` folder into your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Navigate to 'Settings' -> 'Reading Progress Bar' to configure the color of the progress bar.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I customize the color of the progress bar? =

To change the color, go to 'Settings' -> 'Reading Progress Bar' in the WordPress admin dashboard. Choose your desired color from the dropdown menu provided.

= Will this plugin work with my theme? =

The Reading Progress Color Bar plugin is designed to be compatible with most WordPress themes. If you run into any display issues, try adjusting the settings or reach out for support.

= Can I disable the progress bar on specific pages or posts? =

By default, the plugin adds the progress bar to all posts and pages. Currently, there isn’t an option to disable it on specific pages or posts, but this feature may be added in future updates.
